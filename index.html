The Perfect Stain Guide

A rich, dark burgundy stain requires teamwork. Follow these essential guidelines to maximize the color of your organic henna.

Before We Arrive

  • Finish all waxing, manicures, and pedicures a day before.
  • Wash hands thoroughly with soap to remove natural body oils.
  • Do not apply any body lotion or moisturizer.

Aftercare Instructions

  • Keep the paste on for a minimum of 6 to 8 hours.
  • Apply a sticky mixture of lemon juice and sugar to hold it in place.
  • Warm your hands slightly over clove smoke (Loung ka sek).
  • Scrape it off gently. Avoid water contact for the next 12 hours.
